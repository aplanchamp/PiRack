About the project

This website was developed according to project conducted by a 6 member team within ENSEIRB MATMECA during their third and last year. Two members are specialising in RSC (réseau et systèmes embarqués communiquants) whereas the rest of the team is specialising in GLRT (génie logiciel des réseaux et des télécommunications). The purpose of this website is to control a Pirack composed of 48 Raspberry Pi. The aim behind this preliminary work is to create many Piracks and to make them communicate in order to study and test distributed algorithms.
